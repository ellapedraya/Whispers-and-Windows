Poem #1: Stillness Speaks

The world says,
run.
Climb higher.
Be louder.
Shine faster.

But the quiet things—
the steady breath,
the rooted tree,
the gaze that holds
without needing to fix—
they speak, too.

Stillness says:
I am enough,
even when I do not move.

Stillness says:
Peace is not the end of motion,
but the beginning of meaning.

So rest.
Not in surrender—
but in strength.
And know that even in your pause,
you are becoming.
